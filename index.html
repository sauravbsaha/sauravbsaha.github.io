Saurav Bhaskar Saha, PhD

I am a computational biologist by training and profession. Since 2011, my research focus has been on understanding antimicrobial resistance (AMR) mechanisms in Gram negative bacteria, and also facilitating these researches by developing databases and tools. My lab is presently working on chemical genomics and machine learning, and we plan to utlize NGS data to improve our understanding of a jig-saw puzzle called "antimicrobial resistance".
